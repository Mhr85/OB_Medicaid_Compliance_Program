Policy:

Ocean Breeze Associates, L.L.C is dedicated to ensuring a culture of compliance, honesty and integrity. Ocean Breeze has developed a Medicaid Compliance Program to ensure compliance with SSL §363-d and 18 NYCRR Part 521. Such program also reflects Ocean Breeze’s commitment to honest and responsible conduct, decreases the likelihood of unlawful and unethical behavior at an early stage, encourages employees to report potential problems, and allows for appropriate internal investigation and corrective action. This policy directive is intended to adopt these required elements, following the recommended structure of an effective Corporate Compliance Program.

Purpose

The purpose of this policy directive is to codify Ocean Breeze’s Medicaid Compliance Program. Such program is designed to maintain the integrity of the Medicaid program by both preventing and detecting fraudulent, abusive, and wasteful practices within Ocean Breeze.

Applicability

This policy directive shall apply to all Ocean Breeze employees, clinicians/physicians serving under contracts with facilities, paid student interns, and other persons whose conduct, in the performance of work for the pharmacy, including facilities, that is under the direct control of Ocean Breeze.

Definitions For purposes of this policy directive:
1. Abuse

Abuse means provider practices that are inconsistent with sound fiscal, business, or medical practices, and result in an unnecessary cost to the Medicaid program, or in reimbursement for services that are not medically necessary or that fail to meet professionally recognized standards for health care.
